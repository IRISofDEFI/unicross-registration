How to Complete Your Registration

  1. Enter your JAMB Registration Number
    Type your JAMB reg number in the "New Candidate" card on the left and click Proceed to Purchase E-Pin. Your details will be fetched from the JAMB database for verification.
  2. Verify your information & update contact details
    Confirm that your name, faculty, department, and programme are correct, then provide a valid email address and phone number.
  3. Pay for your E-Pin (₦2,000)
    Click Pay Now on the payment page. You will be redirected to our secure payment gateway (Credo). Payment can be made via card, bank transfer, or USSD.
  4. Copy and save your E-Pin
    After successful payment your unique E-Pin will be displayed. Keep it safe — you will need it every time you log in.
  5. Log in and upload your documents
    Return to this page and use the "Already Have an E-Pin?" card on the right to log in. Upload your passport photo, WAEC/NECO result, and JAMB result slip.
  6. Submit your application
    Review your details and click Submit Application. Once submitted your application is locked and will be reviewed during the screening exercise.
